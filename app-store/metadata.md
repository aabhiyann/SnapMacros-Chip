## Description (App Store — max 4,000 characters)

**Snap it. Track it. Own it.**

SnapMacros turns your phone camera into an instant nutrition tracker. Point it at any meal, tap once, and Chip — your AI nutrition mascot — breaks down the calories, protein, carbs, and fat in seconds.

**How it works**
1. Snap a photo of your meal (or describe it in text)
2. Chip analyses the image with AI vision and returns macro estimates
3. Confirm and log — your daily rings update instantly

**Features**

📸 **AI Food Recognition** — Gemini-powered vision analysis identifies meals from photos with confidence scoring (Confident / Best Guess / Unsure)

🎯 **Personalised Targets** — Onboarding calculates your TDEE using Mifflin-St Jeor, then sets protein/carb/fat targets tuned to your goal (cut, maintain, or bulk)

🔥 **Streak Tracking** — Daily logging streaks keep you consistent. Beat your personal best.

📊 **Progress Dashboard** — Weekly macro charts, daily ring progress, and a nutrition history feed

🤖 **Chip** — Your mascot reacts to your meals, roasts your weekly habits, and keeps things fun

🔔 **Smart Reminders** — Optional daily push notifications nudge you to log before the day slips away

⚡ **Portion Slider** — Logged half a plate? Drag the slider to 0.5× and the macros update instantly

✏️ **Manual Override** — Edit any AI estimate before logging. You're always in control.

🍎 **Apple Health Integration** — Reads your active energy to give calorie context (read-only)

**Privacy first** — Your meal photos are processed server-side, stored securely, and never used to train AI models. Sign in with Apple keeps your email private.

SnapMacros is for informational use only and is not a substitute for professional dietary or medical advice.
